About me

Hey! I'm Samrat Gharti, a Computer Science student at the University of Cincinnati with a big interest in using technology to solve real problems. Whether it's building a robot that sorts waste or creating a simple web app to help small businesses manage inventory, I enjoy turning ideas into useful projects.

I'm originally from Nepal, where I grew up in a family that worked incredibly hard to give me opportunities they never had. That's something I carry with me every day — it motivates me to keep learning, improving, and giving back in whatever ways I can.

Right now, I'm exploring different areas of tech to figure out what really excites me — web development, robotics, UI/UX design, maybe even cybersecurity. I'm still early in the journey, but I love diving into new tools, building things, and seeing them come to life.

Outside of coding, I enjoy cooking Nepali food, reading literature, learning languages (currently Japanese!), and connecting with new people. I'm always open to collaboration and learning from others — so feel free to reach out!
